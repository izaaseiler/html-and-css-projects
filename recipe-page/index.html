Simple Raclette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic Raclette combines melted cheese to perfection, optionally followed by your choice of vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 20 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1kg Mini Potatoes
  • 200g Bacon
  • 500g Raclette Cheese
  • 200g Champignons
  • 300g Meat and Shrimps

Instructions

  • Place mini potatoes in a pot and boil until tender.
  • In a separate pan, cook bacon until crispy.
  • Heat raclette cheese in a raclette grill.
  • Add champignons to the grill to cook lightly.
  • Serve cooked ingredients with melted cheese on top.
